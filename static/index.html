Synthy 3

Synthy 3 is a collaborative note sender. It has three parts

  • A button for triggering notes (hold for longer)
  • A dial for pitch*
  • A binary number channel picker.

The channel picker takes a series of long or short presses to mean 0 or 1 in a binary sequence. For example: short press + long press is 2. The channel is the current channel you are both sending notes to, and receiving notes from. To change channel, press in a value and then wait a few seconds.

* - Pitch is based on the 0 - 127 MIDI note frequencies.
